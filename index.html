How to play Photo Puzzle

  1. Select whether you play on a PC or a mobile device.
  2. Use your device's camera to take a picture by clicking the "Shoot" button.
  3. Adjust the number of snippets and difficulty level using the provided controls.
  4. Shake your mobile device or click the "Shake!" button, if you are on a PC, to shuffle the puzzle pieces.
  5. Rearrange the pieces to solve the puzzle.
  6. If you get stuck, use the "Move One Step" or "Solve Puzzle" buttons for assistance.
  7. Once you've solved the puzzle, you can save your picture!
